# Horse Rally

![Horse Rally Screenshot](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Horse+Rally+Game)

Horse Rally is an exciting browser-based horse racing simulation game. Watch as five horses compete for glory in dynamic races featuring variable weather conditions, different track lengths, and obstacle courses.

## Features

-   **Dynamic Racing**: Races can be "Flat" or include "Hedges" (obstacles).
-   **Variable Distances**: Tracks range from 5 to 18 furlongs.
-   **Weather System**: Weather conditions like Rain and Wind affect horse selection and race outcomes.
    -   *Clear*: Standard race conditions.
    -   *Rain*: Increases chance of stumbling/falling and slows the track.
    -   *Wind*: Adds wind force affecting speed.
-   **Detailed Simulation**: Horses have individual stats including pace, stamina, aggression, and surge timing.
-   **Betting Board**: View odds, trainers, jockeys, and recent form before the race.
-   **Local Multiplayer**: Add multiple player names and assign them to horses to compete against friends locally.
-   **Live Commentary**: Text-based commentary tracks the race action in real-time.
-   **Visual Effects**: Particle effects, camera tracking, and smooth animations.

## How to Play

1.  **Open the Game**: Simply open `horse.html` in any modern web browser.
2.  **Setup the Race**:
    -   Use the "Race Card" section to see the current race type and distance.
    -   Add players using the sidebar on the start screen if playing with friends.
    -   Or, just pick a horse from the list to bet on it.
3.  **Start the Race**:
    -   Click "Start Race Setup" or "Start Race".
    -   Watch the countdown and the race unfold!
4.  **During the Race**:
    -   Follow your horse's progress.
    -   Listen (read) the commentary.
    -   Hope your horse doesn't stumble on a hedge!
